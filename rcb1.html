Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)

RCB - A Legacy of Excellence

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a franchise cricket team that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is based in Bangalore, Karnataka.
